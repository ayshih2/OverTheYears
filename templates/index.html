Inspired by The Pudding, here is a data driven analysis of the discography of one of the biggest global artists in the world: BTS. A short introduction for those unaware: BTS, or Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단), is a South Korean boy group who debuted under BigHit Entertainment in 2013 with the album 2 Cool 4 Skool. The group consists of seven members: RM (Kim Namjoon), Jin (Kim Seokjin), Suga (Min Yoongi), j-hope (Jung Hoseok), Jimin (Park Jimin), V (Kim Taehyung), and Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook). Since their debut, they've released BLANK albums (? of which are Korean albums while ? are Japanese albums) and have experience exponential growth with each release. Their first breakthrough was with the single I Need U in Korea while they've been making waves in the US ever since the release of DNA.

They're undoubtably the biggest musical group in the world right now, trailblazers in the music industry, and along with the fame comes the inevitable question: what makes them so popular? The answer is simpler than you think: their music.
